Manage your stuff.

Stockpile is the simplest way to manage your inventory, no matter what you have on your shelves.

Whether you have video production equipment, theater props, or landscaping tools, Stockpile is the way to go.

With Stockpile, you can add any item with a barcode on it to your inventory. Each item has a brand, model, and category. You can use these fields to filter your inventory, making it easy to find exactly the item you are looking for.

Tag Your Inventory

To rent an item, all you have to do is scan its barcode and select a return date. To return items, simply scan them again.

Create kits of items that go together so you don't forget anything when you rent items.

Stockpile is great for teams. Everybody can sign up and download the app to rent, return, and manage inventory.
