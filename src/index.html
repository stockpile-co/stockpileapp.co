Manage your stuff.

Stockpile is the simplest way to manage your inventory, no matter what you have on your shelves.

With Stockpile, you can add any item with a barcode on it to your inventory.

Each item has a brand, model, and category.

Use these fields to filter your inventory to find exactly the item you need, when you need it.

To rent an item, all you have to do is scan its barcode and select a return date.

To return items, simply scan them again.

Create kits of items that go together so you don't forget anything when you rent items.
