Barcodes

To use Stockpile, you will need to put a barcode on every item you want to add to your inventory. You can print your own or order pre-printed barcode tags like our recommendations below.

We recommend QR codes and 2D barcodes.
  • they scan quickly
  • they can store a large amount of information
  • they can be very small

Our recommendations are from My Asset Tag. You can use the recommendations below or browse their website for another option: any barcode they offer will work with Stockpile.

How to Order Tags

  1. Click one of the buttons above to pick a tag type.
  2. Select Qr-code.
    1. Set the starting number to where you left off if you have previously ordered barcodes. Barcodes must be unique within your inventory.
    2. Increase the number of digits beyond 4 if you expect to have ten thousand or more items in your inventory.
  3. Custom only: upload your logo.
  4. Click "Next step."
  5. Select a material. Suggested materials and their costs are listed under each tag type above.
  6. Select a quantity.
  7. Click "Next step."
  8. Optional: add a specialty adhesive and/or accessories to your order.
  9. Click "Add to Cart."
  10. Proceed through the checkout process.
